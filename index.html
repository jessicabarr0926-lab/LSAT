How to use it

A study loop you can repeat every day.

  1. Pick the recommendation. Start with the weakest area the dashboard names.
  2. Drill 6 to 12 questions. Answer, review the explanation, and tag your miss.
  3. Run timing practice. Use a 17-minute checkpoint or a 35-minute section.
  4. Ask for help. Save a question note when the reasoning still feels fuzzy.

Help

How JessiPreps decides what to show.

How is Study Readiness calculated?

It starts from your baseline, then rises when you complete lessons, answer drills, and improve accuracy. Weak areas keep the recommendation focused.

How should I use Blind Review?

After a timed section, review uncertain questions before checking explanations. Write one plain-language rule for every miss.

Why does the app use LawHub links?

Official LSAT questions should stay in LawHub. JessiPreps tells you what to do next and stores your patterns, journal notes, and study plan.
